Tradier Brokerage, Inc. is a FINRA and SIPC registered broker-dealer that provides cloud-based brokerage services to hundreds of 3rd party investment, advisory, and financial media firms, including Options Cafe. Options Cafe has entered into an agreement with Tradier Brokerage, Inc. to provide access to stock and options trading via Options Cafe.

Tradier is integrated into over 100 companies offering technical trading, scanning, options analysis, education, and digital advisors. Because of its technology and focus on active traders, Tradier has today become one of the largest destinations to process Equity and Options trading volume.

Visit https://brokerage.tradier.com or contact service@tradierbrokerage.com for their connected platforms or detailed pricing.
