Here is a list of options strategies we currently support.

  • Put Credit Spreads
  • Call Credit Spreads
  • Put Debit Spreads
  • Call Debit Spreads
  • Iron Condors
  • Reverse Iron Condors

We are adding new strategies every day. If your favorite strategy is not listed please check back in. Better yet send us a message at help@options.cafe and let us know what you prefer to trade. We promise to move quickly on adding your prefered strategy.
